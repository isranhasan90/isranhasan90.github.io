Hello

My name is Isran K. Hasan, and I am from Indonesia. I hold a master’s degree in mathematics with a major in statistics from the Bandung Institute of Technology, Indonesia, fully funded by Indonesia's Ministry of Education. Currently, I work as a lecturer in the Department of Statistics at Gorontalo State University, where I teach econometrics, time series analysis, and modern prediction & machine learning.
Since September 2020 I am also Managing Editor of the Jambura Journal of Probability and Statistics.
